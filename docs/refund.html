The Rebuild (the 38-day community)

No forms. No questions. No "exit interview." You keep everything you wrote.

Refunds go back to the card you paid with, through Stripe, usually within 5–10 business days.

Reply to any email we've sent you, or write to the contact address in your purchase receipt. One line is enough: "I'd like a refund." That's it.
