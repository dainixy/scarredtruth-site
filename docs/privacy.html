Privacy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Plain words, because that's how we do everything here.

What we collect

The quiz: your answers, and — only if you type them — your first name and email address. You can see your result without giving either. Answers are stored securely (we use Supabase) so your result can be saved and, if you asked, emailed to you.

Talk to Zane: the messages you send in the chat, so the conversation can work.

The Rebuild (paid): payments are handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card number. Stripe sends us your name and email so we can let you in.

Visits: we use Cloudflare Web Analytics — it counts visits without ad trackers, cookies that follow you, or selling anything to anyone.

What we never do

We never sell your information. We never share what you write with anyone. We never sign you up for anything you didn't ask for.

Email

If you give your email, you get what you asked for — your quiz result, or things you explicitly signed up for. Every email has an unsubscribe link that works.

Deleting your data

Ask, and it's gone. Reply to any email we've sent you, or use the contact address in your receipt, and say "delete my data." We'll confirm when it's done.

One more thing

This site is for adults. What you share in the Rebuild's private room is seen by the women in that room and by us — that's what it's for — and the house rule is that it stays there.
