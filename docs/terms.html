What this site is

Scarred Truth offers a free quiz, free writing and videos, a chat, and a paid 38-day community program called the Rebuild. It's encouragement and daily practice from lived experience — shared by real people.

What this site is not

It is not therapy, medical care, or professional counseling, and it doesn't replace any of those. If you are in crisis, please reach for more than a website: in the US, call or text 988 — any hour, any day.

The Rebuild

One payment buys one seat for one person for the full 38 days, plus continued access to the room and your materials afterward. The price and everything included are stated plainly on the Rebuild's page. Refunds are covered by the refund policy — the short version: any day of the 38, plus 7 after, full refund, no questions.

House rules (the community)

First names are enough. What's shared in the room stays in the room. No advice unless someone asks for it. Nothing is for sale inside — ever, by anyone. Be the kind of person you'd want beside you on a hard day. We may remove anyone who makes the room unsafe for others; if we remove you, we refund you.

Your writing

What you write is yours. By posting in the community you let the people in that room read it — that's the point — and nothing more. We will never quote you publicly without asking you first.

Adults only

You must be 18 or older to use the chat or join the Rebuild.

Changes

If these terms change, the date at the top changes, and anything already promised to paying members stays promised.
